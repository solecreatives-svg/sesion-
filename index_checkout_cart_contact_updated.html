Our story

Hey, we're Sesion Cafe. We started Sesion with a pretty simple goal: make the kind of fresh, fruity, ridiculously good drinks we wished we could grab on the way to class, work, or wherever the day was taking us.

Every smoothie is blended with real fruit. Every frapp is layered with care. Every milk tea is shaken to order — and yes, our buy-1-take-1 deal really is every single day.

Drop by, grab a seat (or just take it to go), and tell us your name. We'll remember it next time.

— The Sesion crew ☕
